# Images Folder

## Profile Photo
Please add your profile photo here as:
- `shayna-photo.jpg` (or `.png`)

The image should be:
- Square or close to square (recommended: 500x500px or larger)
- Good quality and clear
- Professional headshot

## Company Logos
Please add company logos in the `logos/` folder:

- `hudl-logo.png` - Hudl logo
- `nebraska-logo.png` - Nebraska College of Business logo
- `garmin-logo.png` - Garmin logo
- `nelnet-logo.png` - Nelnet logo
- `werner-logo.png` - Werner Enterprises logo

### Logo Requirements:
- PNG format with transparent background (preferred)
- High resolution (at least 200px wide)
- White/light logos work best on the pink background
- You can find logos on company websites or use their brand assets

### Where to Find Logos:
1. Company websites (usually in footer or press/media sections)
2. LinkedIn company pages
3. Brand asset pages (e.g., brand.hudl.com, brand.garmin.com)
4. Simple Google search: "[Company Name] logo PNG"
