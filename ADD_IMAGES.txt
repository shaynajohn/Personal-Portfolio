  📸 How to Add Your Images
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. YOUR PROFILE PHOTO:
   - Save your photo as: images/shayna-photo.jpg
   - Or rename it to: shayna-photo.jpg and drag it into the images/ folder
   - Recommended size: 500x500px or larger (square works best)

2. COMPANY LOGOS:
   - Add logos to: images/logos/
   - Name them exactly:
     * hudl-logo.png
     * nebraska-logo.png
     * garmin-logo.png
     * nelnet-logo.png
     * werner-logo.png

   Where to find logos:
   - Company websites (footer or press/media sections)
   - LinkedIn company pages
   - Google: "[Company Name] logo PNG transparent"
   - Brand asset pages

   Tips:
   - PNG with transparent background works best
   - White/light colored logos look great on pink background
   - At least 200px wide for good quality

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Once you add the images, refresh your browser to see them!
